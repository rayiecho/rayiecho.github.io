About Me

I build things
that matter
for Africa.

I am Regan Odhiambo Ayiecho — a Mastercard Foundation Scholar at the African Leadership University, Kigali, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering. I co-founded Young Africans Network, a pan-African platform now serving members across 12 countries, built entirely from zero budget on GitHub Pages and Firebase.

Beyond YAN, I am building AfriMeet — a video conferencing platform designed to replace Zoom and Google Meet for African organizations, priced in African currencies, optimized for African internet. I believe Africa should own its digital infrastructure, not just consume it.
