My Story

Who I Am

I am Regan Odhiambo Ayiecho, born and raised in Kisumu, Kenya — driven by an unshakeable belief that African youth deserve access to world-class opportunities through technology and education.

As a Mastercard Foundation Scholar at the African Leadership University, I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering while simultaneously building real-world impact. I am a freelance web developer building websites for startups using AI-assisted tools, an ALX-certified AI specialist and an online tutor reaching students across Africa.

From teaching Mathematics at St. Joseph Ng'ula Primary School to mentoring scholarship seekers at Eaal Foundation, co-founding Young Africans Network and leading marketing at Nuruteck — my journey is defined by service, persistence and purpose.
